Engage with the Royal Engagement

People around the world can't get enough of British Royalty, especially with royal engagements. Better news couldn’t have come for these royal family afficinados because young Prince Harry is getting married.

Prince Harry, 33, recently became engaged to American actress and humanitarian, Meghan Markle, 36. The engagement was announced on November 27th and they’re to wed in the Spring of 2018.

England’s last royal engagement was Prince Charles and Catherine Middleton in 2011. According to the Telegraph, It is estimated that over 2 billion people from 180 countries tuned in to watch the broadcast of the royal wedding, and 237 Tweets were posted every second leading up to the service.
